Lackadaisical Covid-19

Hover over the cards to reveal information about COVID-19

Individual

  • Do not touch your face until you wash your hands thoroughly for at least 20 seconds.

    1. Sanitizing as well will greatly reduce possible exposure.

  • Be sure to clean surfaces that are high contact often and especially when an outside container enters your home.

  • The Center for Disease Control lists the following symptoms of COVID-19 as:
    1. Cough
    2. Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
    3. Fever
    4. Chills
    5. Muscle pain
    6. Sore Throat
    7. New loss of taste or smell

Social

  • Be sure to stay at least 6 feet from anyone near you when outside.

  • Always wear a mask when outside. Be sure not to touch your mask at any time unless you must desperately readjust it.

  • Be sure to throw away your mask (if disposable) or clean and disinfect your masks if it’s reusable.

  • Please stay home if your business is not essential. Don’t hang outside with your friends.

  • If you have any elderly inside your home, be especially distant from them as the mortality rates of the Coronavirus for the elderly are much higher than other age groups.

Business

  • If you are out for essentials, please do not bring any people that will not contribute to the groceries. If possible bring only one person to the market for the whole family.

  • Some essential stores have specific hours for seniors only in order for them to get the essentials they need. Do tell others about this! And to respect these hours as well!

  • Local businesses are suffering in this crisis due to social isolation, especially local restaurants. Be sure to consider them over corporate chains and order take out because they need all the support they can get!
